    How to Play

    • Move your paddle with the mouse (or W/S keys in 2 Player mode).
    • First to 5 points wins a round. Win 3 rounds to win the match.
    • Catch power-ups for special effects!
    • Press ESC to pause, F to toggle fullscreen.
    • Keyboard: 1/2/3 for AI difficulty, M for music, S for sound.
